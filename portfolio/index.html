I'm Lucas — a Level 3 DevOps engineer at Lloyds Banking Group, where I design and operate the engineering capabilities one of the UK's biggest banks builds on: Harness CI/CD, a Backstage developer portal, Terraform Cloud composite modules and an OPA policy stack I own end-to-end — including its full v0 → v1 migration.

My job is making governance feel like a feature, not friction: golden-path build templates, D3 supply-chain attestations, scanning gates with Wiz, Aqua, SonarQube and NexusIQ — all wired in so teams ship fast and compliant. Lately that includes AI-assisted pipeline remediation with Copilot CLI and CI-CTL.

Before platform work I sat on the other side of the counter — triaging BACS, CHAPS and SWIFT payments and supporting clients face-to-face. I build tooling for the human on the receiving end, and I have a Lloyds Customer Heroes Award to show for it.
